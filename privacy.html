Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What We Collect

The Workspace Pro is designed to work without requiring you to create an account or provide personal information. We collect minimal data:

  • Email address — only if you subscribe to our newsletter (via beehiiv)
  • Contact form submissions — name, email, and message if you contact us
  • Anonymous analytics — page views, referral source, approximate location, device type (via Google Analytics)
  • Assessment answers — kept in session storage while you use the quiz. If you choose “Save,” results are stored on your device; if you choose “Share,” answers are encoded in the link you copy.
  • Assessment analytics — on completion, Google Analytics receives the resulting persona plus broad budget and work-type categories so we can improve recommendations. It does not receive your height or pain selection.

How We Use It

  • To send you our newsletter if you subscribe (unsubscribe anytime)
  • To respond to your contact form messages
  • To understand which content is popular and improve the site
  • We never sell your data to anyone

Cookies

We use Google Analytics and Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand site usage. These services may use cookies or similar identifiers and process device, referral, approximate location, and interaction data. You can block analytics using your browser settings or a privacy extension.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics — anonymous traffic analytics
  • beehiiv — newsletter delivery
  • Formspree — contact form processing
  • Cloudflare — site hosting and security

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

Your Rights

You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link in any email. You can request deletion of your contact form data by emailing us. Since we don't maintain user accounts, there's no profile data to export or delete.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach out — we're happy to explain.
