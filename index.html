Why It Matters

Your desk is quietly affecting your health

The average remote worker spends 1,800+ hours per year at their desk. Most of those desks are set up wrong — wrong chair height, wrong monitor distance, wrong lighting. The damage accumulates slowly until it becomes chronic pain.

🦴

86% experience back pain

Poor chair support and desk height are the leading causes of chronic lower back pain in office workers. The right chair can reduce symptoms by up to 60%.

👁️

60% report eye strain

Improper monitor distance and poor lighting cause digital eye strain. Simple fixes — proper distance, task lighting, 20-20-20 rule — make a measurable difference.

📉

$2,000+ in wasted spending

The average remote worker buys 3-4 products that don't fit their setup before finding the right one. Our assessment eliminates the guesswork.
