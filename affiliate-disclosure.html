Affiliate Disclosure

Full disclosure of how this site makes money. No hidden arrangements.

Last updated: June 2026

The Short Version

The Workspace Pro is free to use. We earn money through affiliate links — when you click a product link and buy something, we may receive a small commission at no extra cost to you. This keeps the site running.

As an Amazon Associate I earn from qualifying purchases.

The Full Version

What is an affiliate link?

An affiliate link is a special URL that tracks referrals. When you click one and make a purchase, the retailer pays us a small commission (typically 3-8% of the purchase price). You pay the same price whether you use our link or go directly to the retailer.

Which programs do we participate in?

  • Amazon Associates
  • Select retailer affiliate programs (Branch, Fully, etc.)

Does this affect our recommendations?

No. We recommend products based on research, specifications, and genuine quality. A higher commission rate does not influence our picks. If two products are similar and one pays a better commission, we still recommend whichever one is better for you.

We regularly turn down paid placement offers from manufacturers. If we ever accept sponsored content, it will be clearly labeled as such.

What about products we haven't tested?

We're transparent: we don't have a testing lab. Our recommendations are based on aggregated research — product specifications, ergonomic standards, verified user reviews, and manufacturer data. We clearly state when we're referencing specs vs. hands-on experience.

Your trust matters

If you ever feel we've recommended something that doesn't deserve it, or if you find a better product we missed, please let us know. We take correction seriously.
